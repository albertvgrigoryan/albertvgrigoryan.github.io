Type Armenian

Web application with virtual keyboard for typing Armenian texts on any device. "Add to Home Screen" from your mobile browser menu, if you use the app regularly.

Type Armenian is an HTML5 powered web-app that lets you type Armenian texts on devices that lack Armenian keyboard layouts (e.g. iPhones, iPads, etc.). The letters are arranged in alphabetical order and the full range of Armenian punctuation symbols is present. After you have inputted your text you can automatically search it in Google, YouTube or just select it for copying and pasting anywhere else (e.g. e-mail, sms and so on).

Nothing to install, the app runs directly in all modern browsers. Try?

This app requires Armenian unicode characters support on your device!

Rotary phone

Web app just for fun: fully functional simulator of phone with rotary dialler.

Rotary phone is an HTML5 powered web-app that lets you experience the feel of an old-style phone with rotary dialler. Audio and design attributes have been created upon a real prototype, plunging into an entirely real atmosphere of old style phone calling: latent dial tone, switches for resetting and waiting for dial tone, finger stop, auto back-rotating dialler, all with their unique sounds.

Nothing to install or register, the app brings fun directly from browser. Try?

On devices with no voice call support (other than smartphones) the app is still working, but may return blank or error page, when placing a call!
